Dr. Ilayaraja Kathirkaman

GIS Specialist and Programmer

Ilayaraja Kathirkaman is a Doctorate in "Applied Geology" having ten years of teaching experience, worked as an "Assistant Professor" from Oct 2016 to July 2019 (four years) in the Department of Geology, College of Natural and Computational Science, University of Gondar, Ethiopia, and "Assistant Professor" from July 2016 to August 2010 (six years) in Department of Civil Engineering", Bharath University, Chennai, India. I received my PhD during the year 2009 from University of Madras, Chennai, Tamil Nadu, India. During my PhD, I visited Institute for Research Development (IRD), France as part of my PhD Research on Tsunami Modeling.
